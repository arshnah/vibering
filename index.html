■ What is this

A webring is a collection of independent websites linked together in a loop. You start at one site, click "next", and you are taken to the next member's site, then the next, and so on, until you come back around to where you started. No ranking, no tracking, no algorithm. Just a list of people's own websites, connected by hand.

✎ How to join

This ring is invite-only. You cannot add yourself, and there is no sign-up form.

To join, message arshnah on Discord and ask for an invite. If you get one, you will receive the full step-by-step instructions then.
